About

Pete is a retro-pop producer, multi-instrumentalist, and singer-songwriter based in Chicago, IL, creating dreamy, nostalgic music in their apartment studio. Drawing inspiration from legends like Whitney Houston, Anita Baker, the Isley Brothers and more. Pete’s music is a colorful blend of pop, funk, and 80’s textures that make you feel like you’ve always known the song, even when it’s your first time listening.
